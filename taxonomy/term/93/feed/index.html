It is as simple as:<br /><code><br /> ffmpeg -i in.mp4 -strict -2 -vf "delogo=x=XX:y=YY:w=WW:h=HH:band=9" out.mp4<br /></code><br /> Replace the XX, YY, etc with the location of the logo in pixels, same for WW and HH for the dimensions.<br /> You can make a screenshot of the video and get the parameters from Gimp beforehand.
